About

Shilu (Sheila) Ji is a Registered Acupuncturist and Traditional Chinese Medicine Practitioner from The College of Traditional Chinese Medicine Practitioners and Acupuncturists of Ontario. She had 5 years formal education in both system of traditional Chinese medicine and western medicine. She received her bachelor degree of TCM from Anhui University of Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) in 1982 and obtained her Master degree of Medicine from Shanghai University of TCM in 1990. She was a lecturer and taught Basic Theory and diagnostics of TCM in Anhui University of TCM and also as a TCM doctor practiced in the teaching hospitals. Bringing her solid base of the theory of TCM and rich clinical experiences, she has been practicing TCM for more than 30 years in China and Canada.

Sheila is good at integrating Chinese herb medicine, acupuncture, needle free acupuncture, moxibustion, cupping and Chinese massage (tuina) and more. She specializes in: chronic cough, digestive problem, depression, infertility, various pain like stiffness, frozen shoulder, office syndromes; dystonia, rheumatoid arthritis, depression, insomnia, anxiety, acne, psoriasis, eczema and more.
